# Renal-Cardiovascular Model of XYZ
A physiologically-based mathematical model developed to simulate the interaction between renal and cardiovascular systems.


Developed by Dr. K. Melissa Hallow and collaborators at the University of Georgia, with many contributions from others, including:

Sergey Ermakov, Charles van Brackle, Summer Anjum, Yeshi Gebremichael, Arthur Lo, Jeni Beh, Manoj Rodrigo, Anna Kondic, Henning Schmidt,
